About Blockchain Badges

We want to decentralize education. We believe that you are more than a resume or a college degree. We want to create a secure and trusted place for storing all of your digital credientals.

If you are learning a new skill, have gained a new certificate, or have just earned a degree, we believe that you should be able to store all of your accomplishments in a badge collection. With this badge you can find job opportunities and show the world the skills you have!

At our core, the Blockchain Badge team is a team who believes in the power of blockchan technology to disrupt the world. We see it as a amazing way of verifying the skills people learn online and offline.
